    Project Idea

    Overview :-

    The project idea is to create a ‘fitness’ application based on Australia for mobile devices that excels with a combination of sports, food and blog.
    Sports - The idea is to bring out a healthy and traditional way of being fit for the users for which the application consist of information such as availability of the nearby grounds / players according to the user’s preferred location for them to engage in several sports and also consist of various exercises to be followed.
    Food - In this part consists various recipes to make at home, different diet plans, and has an interesting option for the users to choose from the displayed food options which is awarded as healthy and tasty recipes and connects them to the respective restaurants unlike other fitness application and also allow the users to make orders with the restaurant which will be kept prepared for the users to take-away or the users can directly refer to the restaurant and visit them in person to dine-in directly by easing up the process for those people who tends to hesitate to cook at home sometimes and still wants to keep up with the healthy meal plan, which would serve as a motivation and as well as help the users to adopt a healthy life-style.
    Blog - This serves the main purpose of making the users want to use our application, by maintaining a page for users to share their experience on using our application and how helpful it was, putting up motivational videos, and also provides a local chat box facility for users to talk or share their perspectives to other users which might help them realise that they are not alone in their weight-loss journey or in their difficult times.

    Motivation :-

    Doing regular exercise is essential for everyone under normal circumstances as it helps people live a longer, healthier and a happy life. Although, it is these times like covid-19 makes it not possible for people at any age considering that they workout almost everyday or even barely, who might even find it difficult to follow a proper diet plan due to the frustration, depression or even because of the time they spend mostly at home in-order to stop the spread of covid-19 making it easy to forget the daily routine and leading to stress-eat, that can be caused because of the lockdown implemented with concern to the pandemic. The government of Australia suggests that it is important to stay active in-order to reduce the risk of health conditions like stroke and heart disease, to control gaining more weight, to reduce stress and anxiety caused by this pandemic, and to improve sleep. This application not only provides benefit during the normal times by helping in boosting up the immune system which might be of a great help to an individual to help prepare their body to fight against any virus leading them to fall sick, but also during such difficult times like covid-19 where one doesn’t want to go to gym, health clubs or fitness centres physically but just use our application instead which gives them the strength to be kept motivated. One of the other main reasons to implement a fitness application is the statistics below showing the projected growth of users using fitness applications from 2017 to 2022 which is to be increased from 62.7 million to 87.4 million users.

    SPORTS :-

    For the sports section, there are two main parts: Firstly, a service which allows users to locate sporting grounds and players that are nearby. Secondly, access to online training virtual coaches.

    Nearby Grounds/Players

    The intended function of this service is to assist sporting enthusiasts in finding nearby sporting grounds or courts. As modern smartphones have the necessary GPS capabilities to locate individuals and locations, the app can use this to allow the user to verify if particular grounds or courts are currently vacant or occupied. It would also provide detailed descriptions of sporting venues, by listing the types of courts, whether it is an indoor or outdoor venue, opening hours, public transportation available nearby and contact information about the local club. Each user will also have their own profile, allowing users to locate one another and for finding coaches for improving their skills. The users profiles can also allow users to add each other as contacts where they can communicate with each other using the chat feature mentioned below. Other features that are included in this program include: The latest sporting news, the results of previous matches played both at the social level and professional level, tutorial videos about learning how to play particular sports, as well as upcoming tournament and match fixtures.

    Online Training via Virtual Coach

    The online training service is an implementation of a virtual video session with a specified coach for users looking for training assistance, similar to video conferencing tools such as Zoom, Skype, or FaceTime. Coaches can then provide feedback, perform demonstrations, motivate, and provide advice to the user during their routine, as they would in person. Furthermore, users will be able to set appointments with trainers, with options to choose from such as: Available times for coaches, the type of sport they are participating in or which workout routine they are following, the specific virtual coach they want to work with and also choosing to keep in touch with their coaches via the chat feature.

    FOOD :-

    This part includes three section mentioned as below:

    Healthy Recipe

    This consist of sub-options such as Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks, Drinks, Quick & Easy, Appetizers, Pasta, dietrie friendly menu items and much more including food for specific health problems. As the user selects a particular choice of recipe, they will be provided with information such as the Nutrition Profile, Total time to cook, number of Servings, Ingredients(easily available at most of the stores), Steps (Directions for cooking the recipe) ,Facts(about ingredients), Tips to be more productive and Exchange (If the user can’t find specific ingredient, then they can replace it with other ingredients) along with photos and video to help for the same. The users will not only be able to view these but also review and add ratings, share it, print, or can save as their favourite. They can also follow a professional chef or select latest and popular recipes of their choice for which they want to follow-up the respective notifications. The users will also be provided with a sort button which enables them to have a personalised view of our application.

    Diet plan

    This helps the users to generate their own DIY diet plan or allows them to simply choose from the recommended diet plan according to their needs (gain muscle, lose weight etc..). The Diet Plan will combine the Healthy Recipe we provide and use the recommended ingredients and relative recipes into the diet. The page of Diet Plan will be displayed in the form of calendar memo, and the screen of each individual date will include the Diet Plan already made. Users are flexible to change their Diet Plan at any time. The corresponding Healthy meal will be set up with a link to relative Healthy Recipe. We need to back up some diet plans corresponding to different requirements in the system for users' reference or selection.

    Dine-in/delivery

    Users should enable their GPS for the purpose of viewing the food items sold nearby to their place. The user can choose to order food of their choice and decide to either pick-up or dine-in. Based on the selected option, the application connects them to the respective restaurants which will further lead to a process where the food is prepared and kept ready for pick or the customers can simply order from the restaurant.

    BLOG :-

    The Application’s blog will contain the following:

    Motivational Videos/Stories

    Users can put in their experience / their daily workout routines / anything they feel like sharing to others on how they used to be and how dedicated are they now, their previous lifestyle stories vs new plans etc which would serve as a motivation for other users to follow some routines, tips etc from watching the videos/stories of others.

    Local Chat Box

    Our application provides a platform where users can have a chat in private with other users and experts who are linked to our application.

    Newsletter

    A newsletter can be subscribed by the users by using their emails and names. Users can have access to the daily notifications that are to be sent to their inbox by just subscribing to our newsletter.

    Social Media

    The blog page will also have links of our Application’s social media displayed. Users can just click on the respective links to see how we carry ourselves in the social media and also can have access to special and fun filled content on the social media page which is maintained by our technical team that would serve as an entertainment for our fellow users.

      This includes sections such as About Us, FAQ’s, Contact Support, Terms of Service, Troubleshooting, Delete Account.
